# Flight Spend and Cost Analysis Dashboard
An interactive business intelligence dashboard designed to analyze corporate flight spending patterns, cost drivers, route-level expenses, and departmental travel behavior to support financial control and strategic travel optimization.

## Objective

Provides complete visibility into corporate flight expenses and booking behavior — enabling organizations to monitor spending trends, identify high-cost routes, evaluate departmental travel patterns, and optimize travel budgeting.

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack

| Tool | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| 📊 Power BI Desktop | Data visualization & reporting |
| 📂 Power Query | Data preparation & transformation |
| 🧠 DAX | KPI & cost analysis metrics |
| 🧩 Data Modeling | Relationships between bookings, routes, airlines & departments |
| 📁 File Format | .pbix for development & reporting |

---

## 📂 Data Source

Simulated corporate flight booking dataset demonstrating travel cost management analytics, including:

- Department-wise flight spend
- Airline-wise booking distribution
- Route-level travel expenses
- Origin & destination cities
- Ticket pricing & spend per trip
- Monthly travel cost trends
- Travel class & booking behavior

---

## 🎯 Dashboard Goals

- Track total flight spend and booking volume
- Identify highest-spending routes and cost concentrations
- Monitor monthly travel expense trends
- Compare travel spend across departments
- Support cost reduction and travel policy optimization

---

## 📊 Key Visuals

### 1. Executive KPI Panel

Total flight spend, bookings, average ticket price, spend per trip, and last-minute booking percentage all at a glance.

### 2. Monthly Flight Spend Trend *(Area Chart)*

Tracks expense fluctuations across months to surface seasonal cost patterns.

### 3. Flight Spend by Department *(Bar Chart)*

Compares departmental travel expenditure to identify major cost drivers.

### 4. Highest-Spending Routes *(Bar Chart)*

Ranks routes by total cost to highlight expensive travel corridors.

### 5. Airline Spend Distribution *(Donut Chart)*

Shows booking share across airlines to support vendor negotiation.

### 6. Geographic Travel Map

Visual overview of route distribution across cities for network analysis.

### 7. Trip Type Analysis

Compares one-way vs. round-trip spending patterns.

### 8. Interactive Filters

Slice data by airline, department, travel class, and date range.
