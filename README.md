# Flight Spend & Cost Analysis Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing corporate flight spending patterns, cost drivers, route-level expenses, and departmental travel behavior — built during my internship at TripTrip India to support financial control and strategic travel optimization.

---

## 🎯 Objective

Corporate finance and travel teams often lack a consolidated view of flight expenditure — which routes are expensive, which departments overspend, and how last-minute bookings inflate costs.

This dashboard provides complete visibility into flight expenses and booking behavior, enabling spend monitoring, route-level cost analysis, and data-backed travel policy decisions.

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack

| Tool | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Power BI Desktop | Data visualization & reporting |
| Power Query | Data preparation & transformation |
| DAX | KPI & cost analysis metrics |
| Data Modeling | Relationships between bookings, routes, airlines & departments |
| File Format | `.pbix` |

---

## 📂 Data Source

Corporate flight booking dataset demonstrating travel cost management analytics:

- Department-wise flight spend
- Airline-wise booking distribution
- Route-level travel expenses
- Origin & destination cities
- Ticket pricing & spend per trip
- Monthly travel cost trends
- Travel class & booking behavior

---

## 📊 Key Visuals

**1. Executive KPI Panel**  
Total flight spend · bookings · average ticket price · spend per trip · last-minute booking % — all above the fold.

**2. Monthly Flight Spend Trend** *(Area Chart)*  
Tracks expense fluctuations across months to surface seasonal cost patterns.

**3. Flight Spend by Department** *(Bar Chart)*  
Compares departmental travel expenditure to identify major cost drivers.

**4. Highest-Spending Routes** *(Bar Chart)*  
Ranks routes by total cost to highlight expensive travel corridors.

**5. Airline Spend Distribution** *(Donut Chart)*  
Booking share across airlines to support vendor negotiation discussions.

**6. Geographic Travel Map**  
Visual overview of route distribution across origin and destination cities.

**7. Trip Type Analysis**  
Compares one-way vs. round-trip spending patterns and their cost implications.

**8. Interactive Filters**  
Slice by airline, department, travel class, and date range.
